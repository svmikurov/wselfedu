class task.models.points.Points(*args, **kwargs)¶

User points model.

For the correct execution of the exercise, the user receives points. This model stores the history of the user’s points, their receipt, write-off and the balance of points at the current moment.

user¶

User owner of points.

task¶

The task for which points were awarded.

award¶

Amount of points awarded.

write_off¶

Amount of points written off.

exception DoesNotExist¶
exception MultipleObjectsReturned¶
balance¶

Current balance of points.

Guardianship by virtue of which points are written off.

created_at¶

Date and time of creation of the record in the table.

clean() → None¶

Validate the condition of adding entry.

In one entry you can fill only one of the two fields, either award or write_off.

Raises¶
